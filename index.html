How To Become a Hacker!!!

Lot's of people ask me about this question, 'what do we need to become a hacker?' So here is my suggestions.

1. First of all identify what are the advantages and disadvantages of hacking and how many hackers there are, (types of hackers).

2. Learn programming languages like Python, JavaScript and bash. I prefer Python.

3. Study networking concepts like TCP/IP, Proxies, Socks, VPN, SSL, VPS, RDP, FTP, POP3, SMTP, Telnet and SSH.

4. Learn Linux System. Linux is most important in hacking.

5. Learn about hacking Operating Systems like Kali Linux and Parrot Security Operating Systems.

6. Learn the major hacking tools. The likes of Metasploit, Nmap, Wireshark, Social Engineering Toolkit, etc.

7. Now you are good to go. Go visit HackTheBox and TryHackMe sites to practice.

WATCHING OUT FOR INACTIVE PORTS

Not too long I was working in a classroom when a lab technician came in to replace monitor. The old monitor had long since been removed. He reached around with the cable, plug it in to the back of the computer, and return it on. Unfortunately, the monitor simply give a default message indicating that it was not connceted. He thought that the cable might have been bad, so he went and rectify another cable but still had the same problem. He didn't gave up but was momentarily stumped and asked for my advice. I remeber how easy it is to use the inactive port and wondered aloud if the PC had inactive video port. My eyes lit up and i said to myself, 'Yes' I had seen this before". This time I got on my hands and knees to look at the port and realized that was exaclty the problem. I swapped the cable and the monitor worked perfectly. It is very easy to plug a monitor into the inactive port and even season the techinicain can sometimes be confused by this. If you dont have a display when plugging a new monitor doublecheck the port you are using.
